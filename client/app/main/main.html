Making friends between the cooker and the sink!

An easy way to get to know each other besides university, clubs and bars.

Only for Students.

Sign up with your university email to join the community e.g. sven.svenson@campus.lmu.de

Randomized tables

We will form groups of four tablesurfers. Our matching algorithm prefers mixed-gender groups with different academic background and common preferred weekdays.

WhatsApp Group Chat

Chat about what you want to cook, when you want to meet and what you bring with you. One of you will be chosen as the host who should offer a location for the meetup e.g. in a kitchen, in a park or at a barbecuing area.

Cook together and have fun!
